## üåø Introduction

The life sciences industry has long wrestled with the complexity of its own success: **rich datasets**, **complex regulations**, and **critical demands for speed and accuracy**. In 2025, a new paradigm is emerging to meet these challenges: **agentic AI**.

Agentic AI combines the raw power of large language models (LLMs) with the ability to **plan**, **orchestrate**, and **complete tasks** by interacting with other tools, data sources, and services.  
Rather than acting as a single intelligent assistant, **agentic AI works via a team of specialized agents**, collaborating behind the scenes to complete high-value work.

This bootcamp introduces participants to **agentic AI in life sciences** through a guided exploration and hands-on experience with **CLARA - Clinical and Laboratory AI for Regulatory Affairs**.

## Bootcamp Use Case Overview

During this bootcamp, participants will explore how **agentic AI** can revolutionize regulatory workflows through a **practical, enterprise-grade use case**:  
### **Automating Regulatory Document Generation with Agentic AI**

Regulatory submissions are a cornerstone of the pharmaceutical and biotech landscape ‚Äî and a notorious bottleneck. Preparing **IND**, **NDA**, or **CSR** submissions requires pulling data from multiple systems, ensuring compliance with strict standards, and maintaining traceability.

#### How Agentic AI Streamlines This

1. **Data Retrieval Agent** ‚Äì pulls clinical, non-clinical, and CMC data from backend systems.  
2. **Document Drafting Agent** ‚Äì generates a draft submission report using regulatory templates.  
3. **Validation Agent** ‚Äì checks formatting and compliance against regulatory guidelines.  
4. **Quality Review Agent** ‚Äì ensures consistency, flags gaps, and supports human oversight.

**Result:** submission-ready regulatory documents in a fraction of the time, with fewer manual errors and faster timelines.
